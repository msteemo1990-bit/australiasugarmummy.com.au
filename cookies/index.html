What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on a device when a person visits a website. Similar technologies include pixels, tags, local storage, SDKs, and device identifiers. They can help a site remember settings, support security, understand performance, or operate account features.

Cookies can be set by the website itself or by third-party service providers. The exact cookies used by Australia Sugar Mummy should be documented after the production stack, analytics tools, forms, account system, and security tools are confirmed.
